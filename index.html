Information Fusion Perception and Control lab

Information fusion perception and control lab is directed by Prof. Wen-An Zhang at the Department of Control science and Engineering, College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology. We focus on information fusion， perception and control for autonomous systems. Current research interests contain information fusing, intelligent perception, robotic control and learning algorithms.
